教学理念：用费曼学习法驾驭AI

THE FEYNMAN TECHNIQUE AS OUR AI PEDAGOGY

费曼学习法的核心是“以教为学”，这与提示词工程的本质不谋而合。

1. 锁定概念 Concept

老师明确本节课的数学知识点。

2. 尝试教授 Teach

老师编写提示词，“教”AI如何向学生解释这个概念。

3. 回顾纠错 Review

审视AI生成的作品，如果“教案”（提示词）有漏洞，就迭代优化。

4. 简化提炼 Simplify

不断优化，直到AI能用最直观、最富美感的方式呈现给学生。

课程实践：G3 探索与发现

GRADE 3: EXPLORATION AND DISCOVERY

目标：熟练掌握AI图文生成基础，用AI可视化基础数学概念，激发学生浓厚兴趣。

G3课例 (1): 倍增的魔力

数学概念: 乘法 (3x4)

创作活动: 将3x4视觉化为3组各4个的奇幻生物，或3行4列的宝石阵列。

Ideogram提示词案例

Children's storybook illustration of three groups of fantasy creatures. Each group has exactly four cute, colorful, glowing monsters. The groups are arranged neatly on a magical landscape. vibrant, playful, cartoon style. --ar 4:3

G3课例 (2): 镜子里的魔法

数学概念: 轴对称

创作活动: 创作一幅具有完美中心对称或轴对称的蝴蝶、雪花或图腾。

Ideogram提示词案例

A beautiful, intricate butterfly with perfectly symmetrical wings made of glowing crystal patterns. The butterfly is in the center against a dark, magical forest background. detailed, fantasy, glowing, illustration. --ar 1:1

G3课例 (3): 一半与一半的世界

数学概念: 分数 (1/2)

创作活动: 创作一个半边是白天、半边是黑夜的星球，用“两种风格的融合”来体现比例感。

Ideogram提示词案例

A beautiful planet split down the middle. The left half shows a sunny daytime landscape with fluffy clouds. The right half shows a starry night sky with a crescent moon. The two halves blend seamlessly in the center. fantasy, vibrant, epic art. --ar 16:9

课程实践：G4 精准与风格

GRADE 4: PRECISION AND STYLE

目标：引入并掌握[Constraint]和[Exemplar]提示词，精确控制AI输出，并模仿特定艺术风格。

G4课例 (1): 梵高画笔下的质数

数学概念: 质数与合数

创作活动: 模仿梵高风格，创作一幅画，画面中只有代表质数的向日葵（如2, 3, 5, 7...朵花瓣）。

Ideogram提示词案例 (Exemplar)

A vibrant oil painting of sunflowers in a vase, in the expressive, impasto style of Vincent van Gogh. [Exemplar] One sunflower has exactly 3 petals, another has 5 petals, and a third has 7 petals, all representing prime numbers. The background is a swirling blue sky. --ar 4:5

G4课例 (2): 角度的性格

数学概念: 锐角、直角、钝角

创作活动: 将角拟人化，创作“温和的锐角先生”、“严肃的直角法官”和“懒洋洋的钝角先生”。

Ideogram提示词案例

A cute, personified right angle character dressed as a judge with a stern expression. The character is made of clean, sharp lines, forming a perfect 90-degree corner. The character is holding a gavel. simple character design, illustration, white background. --ar 1:1

G4课例 (3): 埃舍尔的无限镶嵌

数学概念: 平面图形的密铺 (Tessellation)

创作活动: 模仿M.C. Escher的风格，设计一个可以用鱼或鸟的形状进行无缝拼接的图案。

Ideogram提示词案例 (Constraint + Exemplar)

A seamless tessellation pattern of interlocking fish and birds, in the mind-bending style of M.C. Escher. [Exemplar] The color palette should only be black, white, and red. [Constraint] The transition from fish to bird should be smooth and logical. graphic design, intricate, optical illusion. --ar 16:9

课程实践：G5 叙事与抽象

GRADE 5: NARRATION AND ABSTRACTION

目标：引入并掌握[Narrative]和[Emotion]提示词，创作系列图像讲述故事、演绎逻辑，挑战更抽象的概念。

G5课例 (1): 寻宝之旅：寻找X

数学概念: 变量(x, y)与解方程

创作活动: 创作一个至少3幅的系列图像故事：1.发现藏宝图；2.经历重重困难；3.最终找到标记着“X”的神秘宝箱。

Ideogram提示词案例 (Narrative)

A 3-panel comic strip telling a story. [Narrative] Panel 1: An adventurer discovers an ancient, glowing treasure map in a dark cave. Panel 2: The adventurer crosses a rickety rope bridge over a deep canyon. Panel 3: The adventurer opens a magnificent treasure chest, revealing a single, brightly glowing letter 'X' inside. adventure, cinematic, illustration.

G5课例 (2): 情感的色彩

数学概念: 抽象几何

创作活动: 尝试创作“快乐的几何”或“忧郁的代数”等充满情感的抽象画。

Ideogram提示词案例 (Emotion)

An abstract painting expressing the feeling of "joy". [Emotion] Use vibrant, warm colors like yellow, orange, and magenta. The composition should feature dynamic, upward-moving shapes, circles, and swirling lines. In the style of Wassily Kandinsky. abstract expressionism, painting. --ar 16:9

G5课例 (3): 欧几里得的回响

数学概念: 勾股定理的证明

创作活动: 这是一个大型项目，用一个7个图像的视觉序列，清晰、优美地“证明”勾股定理。

Ideogram提示词案例 (CoT + Narrative)

An educational infographic visually proving the Pythagorean theorem. [Narrative & CoT] Step 1: Show a right triangle with sides a, b, c. Step 2: Show squares built on each side with areas a², b², c². Step 3: Visually rearrange the area of the two smaller squares to perfectly fit inside the largest square. Clean, minimalist, educational diagram style.
